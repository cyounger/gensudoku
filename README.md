# gensudoku

Generate 9x9 sudoku puzzles

### Example usage

Generate a sudoku:

```
% gensudoku
seed: 1437232126
2 . . | . 3 . | . . 1
. . . | 5 . . | . 4 .
1 . 5 | . . . | . . .
------+-------+------
. 8 . | . . . | . 1 6
. 7 . | 6 . . | . . .
. . . | . 8 9 | . . .
------+-------+------
. . . | 2 . . | 5 . .
3 2 . | . . 6 | . . .
. 4 . | 8 . . | 7 2 .
```

Generate a sudoku with a few extra hints:

```
% gensudoku --add-hints=5
seed: 1437232464
. . . | . . 8 | . . .
8 7 3 | 4 . 5 | . 9 .
. 5 9 | 6 2 3 | . . 1
------+-------+------
9 8 . | 1 . . | 6 . .
. 6 . | . 5 . | 7 . .
3 . . | . . 6 | . . .
------+-------+------
. . 2 | 9 . . | . 6 .
. . . | . 8 . | 3 5 .
. 3 . | . . . | . 1 .
```

Solve a previously generated puzzle:

```
% gensudoku --seed=1437232464 --solution
seed: 1437232464
1 2 6 | 7 9 8 | 5 4 3
8 7 3 | 4 1 5 | 2 9 6
4 5 9 | 6 2 3 | 8 7 1
------+-------+------
9 8 7 | 1 4 2 | 6 3 5
2 6 1 | 3 5 9 | 7 8 4
3 4 5 | 8 7 6 | 1 2 9
------+-------+------
5 1 2 | 9 3 7 | 4 6 8
6 9 4 | 2 8 1 | 3 5 7
7 3 8 | 5 6 4 | 9 1 2
```
